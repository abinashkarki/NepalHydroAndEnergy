# Purbi Chitwan 132 kV Substation

A proposed eastern Chitwan 132/33 kV substation intended to strengthen supply
for the Chitwan load pocket and reduce the dependence on the Bharatpur-side
single-circuit connection described in the FY 2019/20 annual book.

> [!cite] nea-transmission-annual-book-2077
> Book page 46 presents Purbi Chitwan as a 132/33 kV, 2×30 MVA substation
> project with roughly 10 km of associated 33 kV sub-transmission works.

## Why It Matters

- It shows how local reliability needs were pushing new 132/33 kV nodes even
  outside the headline cross-border corridors.
- It belongs in the same transmission-hardening frame as
  [[hetauda-bharatpur-bardaghat-corridor]] and the wider
  [[nepal-transmission-landscape-2025]].
