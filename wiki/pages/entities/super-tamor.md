## Significance

At 155 MW, Super Tamor is one of the larger survey-stage projects in eastern Nepal, though it sits in the shadow of the much larger [[upper-tamor]] (285 MW, under construction) on the same river. The Tamor basin has attracted attention for both run-of-river and storage development potential, with multiple projects at different licence stages competing for water allocation and transmission evacuation from a relatively undeveloped eastern corridor.
