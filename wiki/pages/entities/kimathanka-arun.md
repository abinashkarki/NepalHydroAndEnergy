## Why It Matters

Kimathanka Arun matters because it extends the Arun corridor story upstream. [[arun-3]] is the near-term, India-developed 900 MW anchor; Kimathanka Arun is a later-horizon state-promoted project that would add another large block in the same eastern basin.

That makes it important for transmission and sequencing. A 450 MW project in upper Arun geography is not just a generation question; it depends on road access, evacuation planning, environmental constraints, and how the broader Arun cascade is prioritized.

## Status And Timing

The current layer treats Kimathanka Arun as survey-stage. The IPSDP milestone ladder groups **Kimathanka Arun** in the **2036-2040** project window. There is no confirmed construction start in the current wiki evidence set.
