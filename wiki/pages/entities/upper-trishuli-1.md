## Why It Matters

Upper Trishuli-1 is not a top-10 capacity project, but it is strategically important because of location. Trishuli projects sit close to the country's central load geography and to transmission corridors that matter immediately for domestic reliability. A 216 MW project in this corridor can be more system-relevant than a larger but remote survey marker if it reaches commissioning and evacuates cleanly.

The project also helps explain why the priority watchlist is not just a biggest-MW layer. Upper Trishuli-1 is included for corridor and buildout relevance.
