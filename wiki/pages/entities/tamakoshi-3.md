## Why It Matters

Tamakoshi 3 matters as a scale comparator. At 650 MW, it is larger than the already-commissioned [[upper-tamakoshi]], but the two projects are at very different delivery stages. The contrast helps the wiki keep capacity and execution separate.

It also matters for basin concentration. Dolakha and the Tama Koshi corridor already carry major hydropower infrastructure; adding Tamakoshi 3 would deepen that eastern-central concentration and increase the importance of transmission evacuation through the Khimti/Tamakoshi grid path.

## Status And Timing
