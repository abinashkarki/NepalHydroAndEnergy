# Kulekhani II

32 MW · operating · on the Kulekhani · in Makawanpur district.

> [!note] **Cascade context**
> Kulekhani II is the second plant in Nepal's only operational reservoir hydropower system. It operates as a cascade plant using discharge from [[kulekhani-i]] (60 MW, reservoir). See [[kulekhani-cascade]] for the full three-plant system overview. Combined generation in FY 2024/25: 279.8 GWh.
