# Nawalpur 132 kV Substation

A planned 132/33 kV substation project intended to improve supply reliability
in Nawalpur and connect back into the Haripur 33/11 kV node through new
33 kV sub-transmission links.

> [!cite] nea-transmission-annual-book-2077
> Book page 54 describes the Nawalpur substation as a 132/33 kV, 63 MVA node
> with additional 33 kV double-circuit line works to feed the local load area.

## Why It Matters

- This is the kind of mid-voltage reinforcement asset that sits between the
  headline 220/400 kV corridors and the distribution-level service outcome.
- It adds detail to the broader story of [[nea]] trying to convert bulk power
  growth into local reliability improvements.
