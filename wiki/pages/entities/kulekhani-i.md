# Kulekhani I

60 MW · operating · on the Kulekhani · in Makawanpur district.

> [!note] **Cascade context**
> Kulekhani I is the upper reservoir plant in Nepal's only operational reservoir hydropower system. See [[kulekhani-cascade]] for the full three-plant system overview including [[kulekhani-ii]] (32 MW) and Kulekhani III (14 MW). Combined generation in FY 2024/25: 279.8 GWh across all three plants.

## Significance

Kulekhani I is the anchor of Nepal's [[storage-deficit]] story: the largest plant in the country's only operating reservoir cascade. As a true reservoir facility, it provides the firm, dispatchable generation that Nepal's RoR-heavy fleet lacks. See [[kulekhani-cascade]] for the full system context and [[firm-power]] for why reservoir capacity matters.
