# Ratmate–Rasuwagadhi–Kerung 400 kV Transmission Line

Nepal's planned first grid interconnection with China in the annual-book 2020
planning frame. The project is both a power-trade idea and a geopolitical
signal: a proposed 400 kV route from the Ratmate side of the Nepal grid to the
Rasuwagadhi/Kerung border axis.

> [!cite] nea-transmission-annual-book-2077
> Book page 65 says the Ratmate–Rasuwagadhi–Kerung line was being advanced as
> Nepal's first power-grid interconnection with China, with a planned
> **~70 km** 400 kV double-circuit line from the Rasuwagadhi border point to
> Ratmate and a power-exchange substation at Ratmate.

## Why It Matters

- This is the clearest 2020-era public example of Nepal trying to widen its
  grid geopolitics beyond the India-only transmission frame.
- Even as a survey-stage line, it belongs with [[rasuwagadhi]] and
  [[hydro-geopolitics]] because the point of the project was energy-security
  optionality, not just another domestic corridor.
