# North Bihar Kosi-Gandak Floodplain

This dependency zone explains why the Kosi and Gandak routes cannot be read as simple blue lines. They pass into one of the most sensitive floodplain and agricultural geographies in the downstream story.

## Why It Matters

North Bihar is central to the geopolitics map because dense settlement, agriculture, sediment, embankments, and flood memory sit directly downstream of Nepal-linked river systems.

## How To Read The Zone

Read it with [[kosi-ganges-merge]] and [[gandak-ganges-merge]]. The zone explains exposure; the merge markers explain route handoff.

## Caveat

This is not a hydrodynamic or flood-risk model. It does not say Nepal controls North Bihar water outcomes. It says Nepal-linked routes pass through a high-sensitivity downstream geography.
