# Padma-Meghna Delta Dependency

This dependency zone explains why the geopolitics map follows Nepal-linked routes into Bangladesh.

## Why It Matters

The lower delta is sensitive to flood timing, dry-season flow, sediment delivery, salinity pressure, and population exposure. Nepal is not the sole driver of any of these outcomes, but Nepal-linked river timing is part of the larger Ganges/Padma/Meghna system.

## How To Read The Zone

Read it with [[ganges-padma-transition]] and [[padma-meghna-delta]]. The transition marker explains route naming and border geography; the dependency zone explains why the downstream context matters.

## Caveat

This zone is strategic context. It is not a distributary map, salinity model, flood model, or Bangladesh water-allocation claim.
