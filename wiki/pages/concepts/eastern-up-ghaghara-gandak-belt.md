# Eastern UP Ghaghara-Gandak Belt

This dependency zone gives context for the western and central Nepal-linked routes entering the agricultural plains of eastern Uttar Pradesh.

## Why It Matters

The zone is included because the Ghaghara and Gandak routes connect Nepal-linked basin timing to dense agricultural and settlement geography. It is a downstream exposure area, not a water-control area.

## How To Read The Zone

Read it with [[ghaghara-ganges-merge]], [[gandak-ganges-merge]], and [[data-layer-future-regulation-scenario]]. The question is how future upstream storage and river timing could matter in a region already sensitive to floods and seasonal water availability.

## Caveat

The polygon is broad and strategic. It is not an irrigation command boundary, crop raster, or flood-inundation model.
