# Gandak-Ganges Merge

This marker explains where the [[gandaki-basin]] route enters the Ganges trunk through the Narayani/Gandak downstream system.

## Why It Matters

Gandaki is a central Nepal route with strong irrigation and plains relevance downstream. It connects Nepal's river timing story to the Bihar and eastern Uttar Pradesh agricultural belt.

## How To Read The Marker

Use this point to understand the central route: Gandaki in Nepal, Narayani near the border, Gandak in the plains, and then the Ganges trunk.

## Caveat

The marker is approximate and interpretive. It does not model canal commands, barrage operations, or local water allocation.
