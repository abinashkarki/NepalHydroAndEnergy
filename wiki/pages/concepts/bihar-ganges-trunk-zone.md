# Bihar Ganges Trunk Zone

This dependency zone marks the shared Ganges corridor where separate Nepal-linked routes become one downstream political geography.

## Why It Matters

The map needs this zone because users can otherwise over-focus on individual tributaries. By the Bihar Ganges trunk, Koshi, Gandaki, Karnali/Ghaghara, and Mahakali/Sharda routes are part of a broader lower-basin story.

## How To Read The Zone

Use it as the bridge between the upstream route merges and the [[ganges-padma-transition]]. It is where individual Nepal-linked routes become a shared downstream trunk.

## Caveat

The polygon is a broad context zone. It is not a model of flow shares, river operations, or local water allocation.
