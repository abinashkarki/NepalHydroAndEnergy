# Bay Of Bengal Terminal

This marker is the terminal reach of the generated route-to-ocean trace.

## Why It Matters

It gives the map a complete endpoint. Without it, the downstream river story visually stops in the plains or delta, even though the strategic route continues through the lower Padma-Meghna system to the sea.

## How To Read The Marker

Use it as the end of the strategic route trace, not as a detailed coastal or tidal model.

## Caveat

The terminal point comes from the HydroRIVERS route trace. It does not represent all distributaries, estuarine channels, or coastal sediment dynamics.
