# Ganges-Padma Transition

This marker explains the naming and political transition where the lower Ganges route enters Bangladesh and becomes part of the Padma system.

## Why It Matters

For Nepal's upstream story, this is where the downstream frame shifts from northern India plains politics into Bangladesh delta dependence. The same route that began as Himalayan basin geography becomes a lower-basin, dry-season, sediment, and delta-stability question.

## How To Read The Marker

Use it as a hinge between the Ganges trunk and the [[padma-meghna-delta]]. It helps readers understand why the Nepal-origin route-to-ocean layer crosses beyond India.

## Caveat

This marker is a strategic transition point. It does not resolve border hydrology, treaty allocations, or local distributary behavior.
