# Bottleneck Hierarchy

Ranked bottlenecks for Nepal's hydropower system, synthesized across all providers. The ranking carries an important nuance: the answer changes depending on whether you ask about deep structural cause or immediate practical blocker.

## The Critical Nuance

- If the question is **"What is the deep structural cause?"** → Storage and timing. The country cannot hold its water, and RoR produces when the system is already long.
- If the question is **"Why doesn't more MW quickly convert into value right now?"** → Transmission and delivery readiness. Generation is racing ahead of grid, and solar will stress substations before reservoirs arrive.
- If the question is **"What changes after the solar audit?"** → Land, interconnection, and BESS join reservoirs as first-tier planning constraints.
- If the question is **"Why can't Nepal fix any of it quickly?"** → Governance and [[buildability]].

This is not a contradiction. It is a more serious systems view.
