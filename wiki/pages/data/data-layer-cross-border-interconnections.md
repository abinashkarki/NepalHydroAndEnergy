# Layer: Cross-Border Gateways

**Explorer label:** International gateways  
**Layer group:** Transmission and trade layers  
**Feature count:** 10

Nepal-side gateway points for cross-border interconnections with status and timeline context.

## What It Represents

This layer maps the Nepal-side gateway points between Nepal's grid and India's grid. It is central to export strategy, import reliability, and the politics of monetizing hydropower beyond Nepal's domestic demand.

## How To Read It

Use it with trade, transmission, and hydropower layers. Interconnections explain why generation capacity alone does not guarantee export revenue: the system needs cross-border capacity, domestic corridors feeding those nodes, and political clearance.

## Caveats

An interconnection marker does not imply unrestricted commercial access. Trade rules, line capacity, synchronization, scheduling, and approvals still determine what can flow.
