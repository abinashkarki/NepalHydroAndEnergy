# Layer: Hydropower - Construction

**Explorer label:** Hydro — building  
**Layer group:** Hydropower layers  
**Feature count:** 180

Generation/construction-license projects, useful for seeing near-term capacity pressure on basins and transmission corridors.

## What It Represents

This layer maps projects licensed to build or generate, which makes it a pipeline layer rather than a current-supply layer. It is useful for seeing where future MW claims cluster geographically.

## How To Read It

Use it beside transmission corridors and basin seasonality. Dense construction clusters can indicate where evacuation pressure, financing risk, and winter-output limitations will matter before the projects become useful system capacity.

## Caveats

A generation license does not guarantee timely commissioning, financing closure, transmission availability, or winter firm energy. Treat this layer as near-term pressure and option value, not delivered electricity.
