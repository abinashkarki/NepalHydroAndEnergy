# Layer: Floating PV Candidates

**Explorer label:** Floating solar  
**Layer group:** Solar layers  
**Feature count:** 5

Reservoir or future-reservoir floating PV candidates with confidence and capacity-band fields.

## What It Represents

This layer shows reservoirs and future-reservoir contexts where floating PV could become a leverage play. The idea is not that floating PV replaces hydro, but that it can use water surfaces and grid-adjacent hydro infrastructure to add dry-season or daytime value.

## How To Read It

Use it with storage shortlist and hybrid siting logic. A candidate is most interesting when it overlaps with storage, transmission access, and a credible reservoir surface.

## Caveats

Floating PV candidates are early-stage and confidence-tagged. Water-level variation, reservoir operations, environmental constraints, anchoring, grid connection, and institutional ownership all matter.
