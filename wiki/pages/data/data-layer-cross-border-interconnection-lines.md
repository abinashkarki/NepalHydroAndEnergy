# Layer: Cross-Border Links

**Explorer label:** Cross-border lines  
**Layer group:** Transmission and trade layers  
**Feature count:** 10

Conservative line/stub geometries for operational, under-construction, implementation-stage, and planned cross-border links.

## What It Represents

This layer draws cross-border links as conservative endpoint connectors or gateway stubs rather than only points. It makes visible the fact that trade is a physical network problem before it is a market story.

## How To Read It

Use it to compare operational, under-construction, implementation, and planned links. It helps explain why eastern, central, and western Nepal have different export and import possibilities.

## Caveats

Planned or implementation-stage lines are not bankable capacity until completed and operational. Gateway stubs are intentionally conservative and do not claim the full India-side route.
