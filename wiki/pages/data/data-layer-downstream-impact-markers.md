# Layer: Downstream Impact Markers

**Explorer label:** Downstream impact markers  
**Layer group:** Hydrology and basin layers  
**Feature count:** 4

Point markers carrying discharge, annual runoff, monsoon share, and impact notes for major systems.

## What It Represents

These markers summarize basin-scale hydrology at strategic downstream reference points. They make the core numbers easier to inspect than a polygon alone: discharge, runoff, monsoon share, and short interpretive notes.

## How To Read It

Use the markers to compare basins by flow scale and seasonality. They work best with basin polygons and downstream-system lines, because the marker gives a data point while the lines and polygons show spatial extent.

## Caveats

A single marker cannot represent all seasonal, annual, or sub-basin variation. Treat values as context for planning arguments, not as a substitute for station-level hydrological analysis.
