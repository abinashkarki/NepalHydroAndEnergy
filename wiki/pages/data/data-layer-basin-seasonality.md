# Layer: Basin Seasonality

**Explorer label:** Wet/dry season flow  
**Layer group:** Hydrology and basin layers  
**Feature count:** 8

Basin annotations focused on monsoon concentration, winter weakness, and dry-season implications.

## What It Represents

This layer makes seasonality explicit on the map. Nepal's hydro advantage is physically real, but its value depends heavily on when water arrives. The annotations help separate annual volume from winter firmness.

## How To Read It

Use it with storage shortlist, operating hydropower, and downstream markers. The key question is how much of the basin's energy or flow is wet-season heavy and what that means for dry-season deficits.

## Caveats

Seasonality annotations summarize complex hydrology. They should be read as planning context, not a replacement for plant-level generation modeling or climate-adjusted hydrological studies.
