# Layer: Solar Plants + NEA 960 MW Awards

**Explorer label:** Solar plants  
**Layer group:** Solar layers  
**Feature count:** 89

Operating solar plants plus NEA 960 MW award anchors, with approximate-location flags where needed.

## What It Represents

This layer combines known solar plants with NEA 960 MW tender award anchors. It makes the solar pipeline visible as actual places, not only procurement totals.

## How To Read It

Use it to see whether solar is spatially concentrated, how close it sits to demand or grid infrastructure, and how utility-scale solar relates to the Terai and existing hydro system.

## Caveats

Some locations are exact DoED coordinates, while others are district, substation, or place anchors. Always read precision fields before treating a point as a site boundary.
