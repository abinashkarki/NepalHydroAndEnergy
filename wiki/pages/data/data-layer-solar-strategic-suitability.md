# Layer: Solar Strategic Suitability

**Explorer label:** Best solar locations  
**Layer group:** Solar layers  
**Feature count:** 6

Strategic suitability bands combining resource value with constraint notes.

## What It Represents

This layer moves beyond sunshine alone. It frames broad areas by strategic suitability, combining resource potential with constraints that affect whether solar can be useful in Nepal's power system.

## How To Read It

Use it when comparing solar build logic against hydropower and transmission geography. It is particularly useful for understanding why quick-cycle solar is a different kind of planning lever than large hydro.

## Caveats

Suitability bands are strategic, not parcel-screening output. They should not replace land, grid, environmental, or procurement studies.
