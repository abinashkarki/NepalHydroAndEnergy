# Layer: Solar GHI Zones

**Explorer label:** Solar resource zones  
**Layer group:** Solar layers  
**Feature count:** 6

Coarse solar resource zones. These are strategic zones, not parcel-level siting decisions.

## What It Represents

This layer maps broad solar resource geography using GHI interpretation. It places solar in Nepal's spatial energy story, especially the Terai and high-insolation zones that can complement hydro seasonality.

## How To Read It

Use it with solar plants, solar suitability, and hydropower seasonality. The question is not simply where sun exists, but where solar can be built quickly enough to help winter deficits and reduce over-dependence on hydro timing.

## Caveats

GHI zones are not project-ready siting. Land access, grid connection, slope, social constraints, and procurement design matter as much as resource quality.
