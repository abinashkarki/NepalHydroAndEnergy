# ICIMOD Koshi Basin Programme

## Summary

ICIMOD's research programme on the [[koshi-basin]], covering downstream impacts, agricultural dependency, and flood dynamics. Supports the geopolitical significance of Nepal's eastern river system.

## Key Data Points

- Koshi basin supports **>40 million people**
- Contributes disproportionately high sediment loads to the Ganges system
- Koshi irrigates ~**500,000–600,000 ha** in Bihar
- The Koshi is known as the "River of Sorrow" in Bihar due to shifting course and catastrophic flooding

## Relevance to Project

Central to [[hydro-geopolitics]] and [[ganges-contribution]]. Also relevant to [[sediment-as-design-constraint]] given extreme sediment loads. .
