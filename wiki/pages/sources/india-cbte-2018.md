# India Cross-Border Trade in Electricity Guidelines (2018)

## Summary

India's regulatory framework governing cross-border electricity trade. Defines approval mechanisms, designated authorities, and conditions for Nepal's electricity exports to India. Critically, includes restrictions on projects with certain foreign investment.

## Key Data Points

- Establishes Indian approval requirements for Nepal power imports
- India explicitly refuses purchase of electricity from Nepali projects with **Chinese equity investment** or Chinese EPC contractors
- Creates the monopsony market structure Nepal must navigate for exports
- Shapes which projects can access the Indian market vs which are locked out
