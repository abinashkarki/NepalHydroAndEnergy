# DoED Licensing Directive 2075

**"विद्युत आयोजनाको अनुमतिपत्र सम्बन्धी निर्देशिका, २०७५"** (Directive on Licensing of Electricity Projects, 2075 BS), issued by the Department of Electricity Development (DoED) on Kartik 7, 2075 (October 2018).

## Key provisions

- **Q45 design discharge mandate:** Projects must size their design discharge on Q45 exceedance ("Probability of Accident Q45 of Hydrological Time Series Data"). Earlier practices used Q40 or even Q25.
- **Q80 exemption:** Projects ≤1 MW for rural electrification may use Q80.
- **Licence fee basis:** DoED charges licence fees on Q45 basis but has allowed Q40 if a PPA is ultimately signed.
- **No amendment since issuance:** The Q45 clause has not been modified, despite growing recognition of climate non-stationarity in Himalayan hydrology.
- **No climate adjustment requirement:** DoED has not mandated climate-adjusted hydrological decrement factors in feasibility studies, despite draft ICIMOD guidelines.
