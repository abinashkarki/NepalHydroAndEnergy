# Likhu Cascade Research Compilation

Comprehensive research compilation covering the 213 MW Likhu hydropower cascade (Likhu-1, Likhu-2, Likhu-4, Likhu Khola A). Compiled from CARE Ratings Nepal issuer ratings, developer disclosures, and NEA transmission data.

## Key data extracted

- **Likhu-2 stranded generation**: 60% PLF in FY 2023/24 caused by NEA New Khimti substation bottleneck (100 MVA transformer), recovered to 91% in FY 2024/25 after 200 MVA transformer upgrade
- **Ownership**: MV Dugar Group with UAE FDI (Mogha Energy DMCC) — not BPC. BPC subsidiary HCEL provided engineering consultancy only
- **Likhu-4 redesign**: Originally 120 MW export project, downscaled to 52.4 MW domestic after cross-border transmission wheeling dispute with India
- **Financial**: Likhu-2 NPR 10,968-11,246 million cost (~NPR 204M/MW), 78:22 D/E, CARE-NP BBB- (Is) March 2026
- **Cascade**: 213 MW cumulative across 4 plants, all sharing New Khimti evacuation corridor
