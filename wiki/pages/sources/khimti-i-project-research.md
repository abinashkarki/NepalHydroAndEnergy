# Khimti-I Project Research — PPA, Generation, Finance, Ownership

Research compilation covering Nepal's first large private IPP: 60 MW Khimti-I on the Khimti Khola. Compiled from Himal Power Limited disclosures, Statkraft press releases, and PPA analysis.

## Key data extracted

- **PPA**: January 15, 1996, 20-year BOOT. USD-denominated at ~US$0.052/kWh initial, revised to US$0.059/kWh, escalated by US CPI. No seasonal rate split. 50% equity transfer to NEA for NPR 1 at year 20.
- **Generation**: ~9,000 GWh total 2000-2025, ~360 GWh/yr average (~103% of ~350 GWh contracted)
- **Ownership transfer**: September 2025 — Statkraft (57%) and Eviny (~26%) sold to Butwal Power Company, making BPC 100% owner
- **Developer**: Himal Power Limited (HPL), founded 1993 by BPC + Norwegian partners
