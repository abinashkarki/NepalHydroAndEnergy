# CARE Ratings Nepal — Sanima Mai Hydropower Credit Assessments

CARE Ratings Nepal surveillance reports and rating rationales for Sanima Mai Hydropower Limited, a 22 MW private IPP on the Mai Khola (eastern Nepal). The Sanima Mai generation data provides one of the most complete multi-year time series for tracking Q-design discharge performance against contracted energy.

## Key data extracted

### Generation vs contracted energy (Q40 design)

| Fiscal Year | Contracted (GWh/yr) | Actual (GWh/yr) | % of Contracted |
|---|---|---|---|
| FY 2076/77 | 121.7 | 116.8 | 96.0% |
| FY 2077/78 | 121.7 | 107.1 | 88.0% |
| FY 2078/79 | 121.7 | 88.8 | 73.0% |
| FY 2079/80 | 121.7 | 103.4 | 85.0% |

The declining trajectory from 96% to 73%, followed by partial recovery to 85%, exemplifies the hydrological volatility facing Q40-designed RoR plants on monsoon-dominated tributaries. Unlike Chilime's glacially-buffered Q65+ design, Sanima Mai's Q40 parameter produces higher variability and lower average delivery relative to contract.

### PPA structure

- Wet season: NPR 4.80/kWh (promotional, escalated to NPR 5.08 after 5 years)
- Dry season: NPR 8.40/kWh (escalated to NPR 8.89)
- 3% annual escalation for 5 years
- PPA year: 2010
