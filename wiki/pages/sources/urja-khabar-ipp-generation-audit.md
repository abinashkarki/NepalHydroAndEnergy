# Urja Khabar "Audit" of IPP Generation vs Contracted Energy

Clarification of the widely-cited Urja Khabar generation audit. The source is not an independent Urja Khabar dataset or audit report — it is a January 2026 Urja Khabar magazine article by Kumar Pandey citing a MoEWRI report from Ashad 11, 2077 BS (mid-2020) on "financially distressed" small hydropower projects.

## Key data extracted

- **Aggregate findings**: 13 projects producing < 50% of contracted energy; 40 projects < 70%; 50 projects ~80%; 10 projects about 80%. "No project produces more than 80% of design energy."
- **Source chain**: Kumar Pandey (Urja Khabar, Jan 2026) → MoEWRI report (Ashad 2077 / mid-2020). Secondary reporting, not an independent primary dataset.
- **32% figure**: Unattributable to Urja Khabar directly — likely a dry-season performance figure circulating in industry commentary, not an annual average. The wiki's [UNDER REVIEW] flag on this figure is appropriate.
