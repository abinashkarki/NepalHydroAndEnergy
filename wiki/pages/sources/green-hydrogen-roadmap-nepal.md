# Green Hydrogen Roadmap for Nepal

## Summary

Strategic roadmap for producing green hydrogen from Nepal's surplus monsoon hydropower via electrolysis. Envisions an entirely new domestic industrial ecosystem absorbing stranded wet-season power.

## Key Data Points

- Primary target: domestic green **ammonia/fertilizer** production (Nepal imports 100% of chemical fertilizers)
- A domestic green fertilizer plant could absorb up to **500 MW** of continuous baseload power
- Replacing coal in Nepal's cement clinker production would require **227,743 tonnes** of green hydrogen annually, needing **2,002 MW** of dedicated hydropower
- Projected viable production cost: **$2.15/kg** by 2035
- Government adopted Green Hydrogen Policy in 2024 with tax exemptions and regulatory framework
- Commercial pilots targeted for 2025–2027, industrial scale by 2035
