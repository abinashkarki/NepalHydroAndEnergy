# WECS Energy Sector Synopsis Report 2024

## Summary

Covers Nepal's full energy balance for FY 2079/80 (~2022/23), including all energy sources (not just electricity). Provides the broader context for where grid electricity fits within Nepal's total energy system.

## Key Data Points

- Nepal had **3,349 MW installed hydropower** by FY 2023/24
- Generated **9,940.86 GWh** hydro in FY 2023/24
- Grid electricity was only **4.96%** of total final energy consumption in 2022
- Final energy consumption by sector (2022):
  - Residential: 323,463 TJ (60.75%)
  - Industry: 111,306 TJ (20.91%)
  - Transport: 55,553 TJ (10.43%)
  - Commercial: 26,835 TJ (5.04%)
  - Agriculture: 5,040 TJ
  - Construction/mining: 10,222 TJ
- Transmission system: 5,742 circuit-km at 66 kV+; 8,867 MVA substation capacity

## Relevance to Project

The 4.96% grid-electricity share is one of the most revealing data points in the entire project — it shows that Nepal's electricity story is far more advanced than its total energy transition.
