# NEA Transmission Annual Book 2077

## Summary

Official Nepal Electricity Authority annual-book publication for FY 2019/20
(B.S. 2077). The volume mixes directorate narrative, project status notes,
site/substation photographs, appendix tables, and map pages. For the wiki,
its highest-value material is in the transmission chapter and appendix:
project-by-project line/substation status, existing substation capacity, and
under-construction high-voltage grid inventory.
